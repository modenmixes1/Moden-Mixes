About Us

Moden Mixes is a certified kosher mobile bartending company offering top-tier service for any event. Our licensed bartenders specialize in elegant and modern cocktail experiences tailored to your needs. Whether you're planning a wedding, bar/bat mitzvah, corporate gathering, fundraiser, or private party, we bring the bar to you with style.

Services

Weddings: Signature cocktails, champagne service, and stylish setups.

Bar/Bat Mitzvahs: Custom non-alcoholic bars and themed drinks for kids and adults.

Corporate Events: Professional cocktail service to impress clients and team members.

Fundraisers: Elegant drink stations that support your mission while keeping it classy.
