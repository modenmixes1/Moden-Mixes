About Moden Mixes

Moden Mixes is Baltimore’s premier mobile bartending company, dedicated to delivering a five-star cocktail experience wherever you celebrate. We are certified kosher, fully insured, and staffed by licensed bartenders who bring expertise, elegance, and a touch of luxury to every event.

Our team manages every detail-from setup to clean-up-so you can relax and enjoy your occasion. Whether you’re hosting a lavish wedding, a corporate gala, or a cozy private party, we tailor our service to fit your vision and elevate your guest experience.

Our Services

  • Weddings & Engagements: Elegant bar setups, custom menus, and seamless service for your special day.
  • Bar/Bat Mitzvahs: Family-friendly mocktails, creative non-alcoholic bars, and interactive drink stations.
  • Corporate Events: Professional bartending that impresses clients and colleagues, with flexible packages for any size gathering.
  • Private Parties & Milestone Celebrations: From birthdays to anniversaries, we bring the bar and the flair to you.
  • Fundraisers & Community Events: Responsible, stylish service that enhances any occasion.

Service Area: Baltimore, Washington D.C., Northern Virginia, and surrounding regions.

FAQs

  • Are you certified kosher?
    Yes! All barware and ingredients are kosher, and we can accommodate additional requirements upon request.
