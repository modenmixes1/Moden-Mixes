About Us

Moden Mixes is Baltimore's first certified kosher premier mobile bartending service founded by Gershon Moses. We bring sophistication and sparkle to your events with creative cocktails and flawless service. Certified kosher, we combine tradition with luxury to create unforgettable celebrations.

Our team believes every event deserves a signature experience. From intimate gatherings to grand weddings, we deliver excellence, creativity, and hospitality.

Why Choose Moden Mixes?

  • Certified kosher service
  • Experienced, friendly, and certified bartenders
  • Premium ingredients and elegant presentation
  • Stress-free setup and cleanup
  • Flexible packages for any event size

Our Services

  • Weddings & all other Simchas: Celebrate with curated cocktails and impeccable service for your special day.
  • Corporate Events: Impress clients and colleagues with a stylish bar and professional staff.
  • Private Parties: Birthdays, anniversaries, Bar/Bat Mitzvahs - we handle the drinks, you enjoy the party!
  • Mocktails & Family Events: Delicious non-alcoholic options for all ages and occasions.
