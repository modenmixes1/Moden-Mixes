About Us

Modern Mixes is a premier mobile bartending service founded by Gershon Moses. We bring sophistication and sparkle to your events with creative cocktails and flawless service. Certified kosher, we combine tradition with luxury to create unforgettable celebrations.

Our team believes every event deserves a signature experience. From intimate gatherings to grand weddings, we deliver excellence, creativity, and hospitality.

Why Choose Modern Mixes?

  • Experienced, friendly, and certified bartenders
  • Premium ingredients and elegant presentation
  • Fully certified kosher service
  • Stress-free setup and cleanup
  • Flexible packages for any event size

Our Services

  • Weddings & Simchas: Celebrate with curated cocktails and impeccable service for your special day.
  • Corporate Events: Impress clients and colleagues with a stylish bar and professional staff.
  • Private Parties: Birthdays, anniversaries, mitzvahs - we handle the drinks, you enjoy the party!
  • Mocktails & Family Events: Delicious non-alcoholic options for all ages and occasions.

How It Works

  1. Consultation: Share your vision and preferences.
  2. Setup: We bring the bar, glassware, and essentials.
  3. Service: Professional bartenders serve with style.
  4. Cleanup: We leave your venue spotless.
